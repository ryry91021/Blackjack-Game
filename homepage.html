Welcome to the Blackjack Game

Blackjack is an exciting card game where the goal is to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over. Play against the dealer and see if you can beat the odds!

Instructions:

  • Click "Start Game" to begin playing.
  • Click "Hit" to draw a new card or "Stay" to end your turn.
  • Try to get a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer without going over!
